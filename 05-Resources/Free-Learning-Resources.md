## üì• HOW TO DOWNLOAD & ORGANIZE

### Download Strategy
1. Create folder: `C:\LearningResources\`
2. Subfolders for each category
3. Use descriptive filenames
4. Keep a resource inventory in Notion

### PDF Management
- **Calibre**: Free ebook manager
- **Sumatra PDF**: Lightweight reader
- **Obsidian PDF Plugin**: Annotate in your vault

### Video Courses
- **YouTube-DL**: Download YouTube videos for offline
- **VLC Player**: Watch at 1.5-2x speed for efficiency
